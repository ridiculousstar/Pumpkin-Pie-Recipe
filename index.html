Pumpkin Pie

pumpkin Pie

Ingredients

  • 1 (9 inch) single crust homemade pie crust (or store-bought): 1 (9 inch) single crust homemade pie crust
  • 3 large eggs: 3 large eggs
  • 1/2 cup (100g) granulated sugar: 1/2 cup (100g) granulated sugar
  • 1/3 cup (65g) light brown sugar: 1/3 cup (65g) light brown sugar
  • 1 (15oz) can of pure pumpkin puree or 2 cups (440g) homemade pumpkin puree: 1 (15oz) can of pure pumpkin puree
  • 3/4 cup (175ml) heavy whipping cream: 3/4 cup (175ml) heavy whipping cream
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract: 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 ½ teaspoons ground cinnamon: 1 ½ teaspoons ground cinnamon
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground ginger: 1/2 teaspoon ground ginger
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground cloves: 1/4 teaspoon ground cloves
  • 1/4 teaspoon fine sea salt: 1/4 teaspoon fine sea salt
  • Homemade whipped cream, for serving: Homemade whipped cream

    • Instructions

    1. Roll out the pie crust to fit a 9-inch pie dish. Place the crust into the dish and trim the edges to within 1 inch of the dish. Refrigerate while preparing the filling. Preheat the oven to 425°F (220°C).
    2. In a large bowl, beat the eggs and pumpkin puree together. In a separate bowl, combine the sugar, cinnamon, salt, ginger, and cloves, and add to the pumpkin mixture.
    3. Gradually stir in the heavy cream and vanilla extract. Pour the mixture into the prepared pie crust.
    4. Bake at 425°F (220°C) for 15 minutes. Reduce the temperature to 350°F (175°C) and continue baking for 40-50 minutes, or until a knife inserted near the center comes out clean. Allow the pie to cool completely on a wire rack before serving.
